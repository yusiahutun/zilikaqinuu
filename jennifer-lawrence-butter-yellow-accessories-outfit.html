Jennifer Lawrence Brightens an Off-Duty Look With Yellow Accessories

June 2024 Â· 2 minute read

If red was the color of winter and fall, butter yellow is the hue of spring and summer. Recent runway collections from the likes of Bevza, Sandy Liang, JW Anderson, and Burberry dictated it as such, and now, It girls have followed.

This week, Jennifer Lawrence was the A-lister on our radars who embraced the sunny color. The actor was photographed out in New York City in a layered look perfect for transitioning from cool to warm weather.

The base of the fit was an easy tank in a dreamy buttery yellow, and classic straight-leg blue jeans from La Ligne. Over the tank, Lawrence wore a sweet little black sweater buttoned only at the top, and a long trench coat in a greenish-gray tone.
