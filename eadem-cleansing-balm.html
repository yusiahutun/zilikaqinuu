EADEM Dew Dream Cleansing Balm Review 2022

July 2024 · 3 minute read

I don't wear a ton of makeup on a regular basis—I'm consistently a no-makeup-makeup kind of girl—but what I use to remove my long-wearing mascara, brow pencil, blush, and concealer is just as important to me as my minimalist rotation of products.

Although I swear by my holy grail makeup favorites, most beauty enthusiasts can agree that any great look starts with a consistent skin care routine, especially one that's equal parts effective, quick, and easy. I've kept a top-rated cleansing oil in my regimen for years, alongside my go-to face wash, to ensure thorough product removal by way of the double cleanse.

Though I appreciate the deep-clean result I get from my tried-and-true removal process (as does my pillowcase and finicky combination skin), tacking on an extra skin care step at the end of the day sometimes feels like a chore—which is why I'm over the moon about EADEM's new Dew Dream Cleansing Balm.

EADEM Dew Dream Hydrating Cleansing Balm

EADEM Dew Dream Hydrating Cleansing Balm

Credit: EADEM

Unlike other cleansers I've tried before, this jelly-to-oil-to-milk formula washes away lingering makeup, dirt, and oil in one fell swoop, and even passes the towel wipe test for residue when I dry my face. The balm's bouncy texture doesn't feel filmy or sticky on my skin—plus, it has a subtle, uplifting lemon scent that I love.

After massaging it over my face for 60 seconds per the product's instructions, a simple rinse is all that's needed for a completely refreshed complexion. Plus, I can keep my eyes open while applying the balm without fear of burning or irritation—it's that gentle. And just like many of my beloved oil cleansers, the Dew Dream doesn't leave my face feeling tight or dry after using it. It combines a blend of ingredients like tiger grass, allantoin, Kalahari melon, and moringa to nourish skin while minimizing hyperpigmentation—a bonus of all EADEM products.

I'm not alone in my love for this multitasking cleanser; released at the end of July, it's already racked up more than 1,500 loves from Sephora customers, with nearly a five-star rating. "I tried out the hydrating balm on a day that I wore full-coverage makeup, and it performed better than I expected," one satisfied shopper writes. "It removed my makeup completely. I do like that it's in a tube instead of a jar, so application is a bit easier. The texture is interesting; it doesn't feel like I'm applying grease to my face—it actually felt pretty moisturizing."

Traditional oil cleansers will always hold a special place in my heart, but now that I've discovered EADEM's latest one-and-done skincare gem, it's definitely earned a spot on my list of all-time (and everyday) favorites.

Shop EADEM's Dew Dream Hydrating Cleansing Balm online at eadem.co and sephora.com.
