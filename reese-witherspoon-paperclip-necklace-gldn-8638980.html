Reese Witherspoons Go-To Necklace Style Is Perfect for Layering

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Jewelry is one of the easiest ways to elevate outfits. Some people might reach for statement-making pieces while others rely on more simple accessories, but no matter your preference, having a few staple dainty necklaces and bracelets is a must—and it looks like Reese Witherspoon feels the same way. 

Recently, I noticed the same paperclip necklace making an appearance in multiple photos of Witherspoon, and I was immediately drawn to the versatile nature of the piece. I love that it can be stacked with more necklaces without looking clunky or heavy, but it also looks great when worn on its own. While it’s not clear which brand Witherspoon’s necklace is from, I found very similar items from GLDN starting at $52. 

Paperclip Jewelry From GLDN 

Bold Paperclip Chain

The paperclip necklace showing up in Witherspoon’s posts has thicker links, similar to this Bold Paperclip Chain from GLDN. This necklace comes in 14kt gold fill or sterling silver, and the chain can be purchased in three lengths, including 18, 20, and 22 inches. Shoppers agree that it’s an ideal necklace if you’re looking for versatility, with one customer saying that they “wear it layered”, and another shopper added that they love the necklace because it is “dainty yet sturdy.” 

Dainty Paperclip Chain

For a thinner version, the brand also carries the Danity Paperclip Chain, which comes in 14kt gold fill and sterling silver as well. While this necklace is available in two adjustable sizes, you can order the chain in a custom length up to 24 inches long. There are multiple versions of the paperclip chain offered at GLDN, and the Dainty necklace is smaller than the Small Paperclip Chain but bigger than the Mini Paperclip Chain. Shoppers shared that it’s the “perfect layering necklace,” since the “chain is not too big” and “not too small.” 

Bold Paperclip Chain Bracelet

You can still achieve the paperclip chain look even if you’re a bracelet wearer with the Bold Paperclip Chain Bracelet, which comes in both 14kt gold fill and sterling silver. This timeless piece comes in two sizes, but you can clasp the hook anywhere along the bracelet to customize the length. One shopper said that it’s a great item to give as a gift for “milestone birthdays,” adding that it’s a “hit every time.”

Whether you’re looking for an almost-exact replica of Witherspoon’s necklace or want to try out the paperclip necklace style in a daintier version, GLDN has so many pieces to choose from. I included more options from the brand below.
