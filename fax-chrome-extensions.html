The 4 Best Chrome Extensions to Send and Receive a Fax

May 2024 · 3 minute read

While certainly not commonplace, some organizations may still request faxes. If you need to send a fax urgently, the last thing you want to do is spend more time looking for a fax machine. Whether you need to send a fax to submit an important document, or to reach out to one of your contacts, you no longer need to do it physically.

Instead, you can use a Chrome extension to send a fax without a fax machine. Here, we’ll give you the some of the best Chrome extensions to send and receive a fax.

1. Fax.Plus

If you want to send a fax to different countries across the globe, this Chrome extension is for you. With Fax.Plus, you’ll get a fax number that lets you receive a fax from over 40 countries. You can also use the Chrome extension to send a fax to over 180 countries.

You can use Fax.Plus for free to send faxes, but if you want a number to receive them as well, you’ll have to upgrade your account. You can choose between four payment plans, starting from $6.99 up to $79.99 per month. The plan you choose depends on how many perks you need, as well as the amount of fax pages you want to use per month.

2. CocoFax

If you’re looking for an easy way to send a fax online, this is it. With CocoFax, you can send a fax from Gmail and other channels, including your cell phone, Chrome, Google Drive, and more.

With CocoFax, you can opt for the Business or Enterprise pricing package to be able to share an account with team members. That way, more than one person can send faxes using the same account.

You can try out CocoFax by accessing the 14-day free trial. Thereafter, you can use up to 200 pages per month for $6.99 per month on the Basic package. If you require more features, you’ll need to upgrade your package.

3. WiseFax

WiseFax is an online fax service that lets you send faxes from your computer to any fax number across the globe. With WiseFax, you don’t need to sign up for a monthly or yearly subscription service, as you can simply pay as you go.

This service allows you to simply send a fax by uploading your document, selecting the recipient's location, entering their fax number, purchasing your fax tokens. While you don’t need to subscribe to send a fax, if you need a fax number, you’ll need to subscribe for a weekly plan for $4.25, or a monthly plan for $8.

4. Free SMS, Fax Receiver

If you don’t want to subscribe to a payment plan to receive faxes online, this Chrome extension is exactly what you need. With this Chrome extension, you can receive faxes without registering or using your fax number. When you receive faxes, you’ll get to save them onto your devices as PDF documents.

The only downside to using this extension, is that you can not send faxes, making it most suitable for users who only wish to receive faxes. Additionally, you can also use the free SMS number to receive free SMS.

Send and Receive Faxes Online

Chrome extensions make completing certain tasks much easier, and now, it’s even better that you can use them to send and receive a fax from multiple countries across the globe.

You no longer have to dig into your pockets for a pricey fax machine, as you can fax from your personal computer. That’s not all, sending and receiving faxes online doesn’t end with Chrome extension. If you’re looking for other ways to fax online, there’s still more where that came from.
