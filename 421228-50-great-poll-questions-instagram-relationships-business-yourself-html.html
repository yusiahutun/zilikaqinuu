50+ great poll questions to ask on Instagram about relationships, business, yourself

August 2024 · 8 minute read

Since Instagram launched its new poll feature to add to Instagram stories, fans have been gifted with a whole new world of engagement. The great poll questions to ask on Instagram allow you to survey your followers, find out their likes and dislikes, and ask more interactive questions. This feature can help you find out exactly what your followers want to see on your feed.

On Instagram, engagement is everything. More engagement means more growth. If you have been using Instagram but you are having a difficult time engaging your followers, you need to try creating polls. By asking questions, you're giving your followers a unique opportunity to interact and engage on your profile, page, and stories.
