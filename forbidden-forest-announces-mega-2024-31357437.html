Forbidden Forest announces mega 2024 lineup including Wilkinson and Sub Focus

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Forbidden Forest - arguably the most colourful, eclectic dance music festival in the UK, is returning in 2024 with a lineup bigger than ever.

Following a sellout show earlier this year, Forbidden Forest have announced the first phase of its impressive lineup including Wilkinson, Sub Focus, Shy FX, Hot Since 82, Patrick Topping and many, many more - see the full list of massive names here.

Last year 30,000 people descend on Belvoir Castle for the festival’s biggest edition to date and plans for next year look to be even bigger. New additions include incredible, immersive production which will take the show to the next level.

The VIP area will be expanding to be situated against the picturesque backdrop of the lake, plus earthy new stage builds buried amongst the woodland and yoga classes for campers.

Campers can arrive from Friday afternoon to get set up before the sun sets and the magic begins and Forbidden is also working with its sustainable travel partner, Big Green Coach, which will offer coach transport directly to the festival from key UK cities.

Forbidden Forest's owner James Cohen has said: "We can’t wait to welcome our Forbidden Family back to the Forest next year. We’ve raised the bar even further from both a production and lineup point of view and we can’t wait for you to experience what we have created”.

Branded "the Midland's biggest electronic dance music festival" and described as "a beautiful festival with an unbeatable atmosphere" by festival goers in 2023, Forbidden Forest 2024 is the perfect way to kickstart your summer.

The event will take place 31 May to 2 June 2024 and festival goers can find tickets here.

Presale tickets go on sale 10am Wednesday 8 November and pre-registered festival goers will have first access to secure their tickets from just £8.50 plus booking fee online at https://www.forbidden-forest.co.uk/tickets.

Tickets can be purchased via Paylogic, Skiddle or Big Green Coach and payment plans are available.
