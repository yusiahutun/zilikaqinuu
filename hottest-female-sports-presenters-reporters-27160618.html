Hottest female sports presenters - from Iker Casillas' gorgeous ex to NFL stunner

June 2024 Â· 4 minute read

Sports fans tune in to broadcasts for the sole purpose of watching the action on the pitch, court or wherever it may be.

But TV companies have plowed millions into making the pre and post-match content they deliver must-see too, which has seen an influx of new faces on our screens.

Often when we tune in nowadays the presenter we see hosting the show is a female. And not only do they have the brains, but they also offer viewers the added bonus of beauty too, with Daily Star Sport taking a look at some of the hottest hosts around.

Sara Carbonero

While working for Spanish channel Telecinco, Sara Carbonero went viral for kissing Spain goalkeeper Iker Casillas during an interview at the 2010 World Cup. The pair were dating at the time and then married in 2016, but broke up last year after having two sons together.

Carbonero, who was voted 'The Sexiest Reporter in the World' by the American edition of magazine FHM in 2009, sparked rumours they had rekindled their romance when she posted a picture of the former Real Madrid star to Instagram on his recent 41st birthday, but they remain apart.

Michelle Beisner

Before turning to presenting, Michelle Beisner was a cheerleader for the Denver Broncos, so it's no surprise to see her on the list. She switched sports early in her broadcast career as host of "The Turbo Tour".

But NFL was always her calling and Beisner now works as part of the Monday Night Countdown team which airs ahead of Monday Night Football. The 45-year-old, who has worked for various American companies, has wowed not only viewers but also Fox Sports announcer Joe Buck, who she married in 2014.

Diletta Leotta

Italian football fans are treated to the sight of Diletta Leotta on their screens. The law graduate has anchored DAZN's coverage of the Serie A since the 2018/19 season.

Prior to that she presented Serie B games on Sky Sports Italy with Gianluca Di Marzio and Luca Marchegiani. Her celebrity has risen drastically in the years since, with an Instagram following of 8.2million testament to that.

Leotta, 30, embraced her beauty by entering and winning the "Miss Elegant" crown in a Miss Italy beauty contest before her career in TV began.

Charlie Webster

Englishwoman Charlie Webster has been the broadcast equivalent of a football journeyman, having worked for almost every outlet under the sun. Her career began in Spain working for Real Madrid TV.

She then moved to Asia for to present ESPN's coverage of the Premier League and has since gone on to appear on BBC, ITV, Channel 4 and Sky Sports in the UK. Away from sport, Webster showed off her figure in a lingerie shoot for FHM in 2004 and has worked as a campaigner, now holding several ambassador roles.

Charissa Thompson

Charissa Thompson has been keeping Fox Sports viewers entertained since 2013. The former SportsNation co-host had jumped ship from rivals ESPN.

She has had several other broadcasting roles, but sports has always remained at the forefront of her professional and personal life, having married agent Kyle Thousand in December 2020 and also previously dating former Chicago Bulls player Jay Williams.

Erin Andrews

One of the most famous sports broadcasters, Erin Andrews has been blessing broadcasts with her presence for more than 20 years now.

Like Thompson, she also left ESPN to join Fox, who she has worked for since 2012. She was voted 'America's sexiest sportscaster' in 2007 and 2008 by Playboy magazine.
