UFC: Mike Perry posts graphic video lying in pool of blood next to glass pane no matter what

February 2024 · 2 minute read

UFC welterweight Mike Perry has posted a shocking video of himself lying on the floor with his leg bleeding profusely, having presumably kicked a glass pane.

Perry (14-7) shared an Instagram story of his face – lifting his hat, sighing and saying, “Oh [expletive]” – before panning to a shattered glass door and his left leg in a puddle of blood.

In the video, the 29-year-old then lifts his right leg away from the blood and says, “Bro, what the [expletive] am I supposed to do about this?”

***WARNING: GRAPHIC IMAGES BELOW***

“Platinum”, who lost to promotion veteran Tim Means via decision at UFC 255 last November, then posted a tweet.

“No matter what I do it’s never enough. So I act out. Happy to spill my own blood,” said the American, who last won in a unanimous decision victory over Mickey Gall in June 2020.

A month later, video footage released appeared to show Perry punching an elderly man in the face after a verbal altercation at a restaurant in Texas. He told someone to call the police and directed a racial slur at the man multiple times.

In October, Perry’s ex-wife Danielle Nickerson told MMA Junkie that she was physically assaulted by him “a handful of times” last February, amid other allegations. MMA Junkie said it obtained audio of Nickerson’s 911 call, as well as another police recording and court records supporting her accounts of Perry’s alleged abuse.

Before his tweet on Monday, Perry had gone on a social media tirade against MMA fans, explaining why he did not like talking to or taking pictures with them.

“[I don’t care], the fans suck, people suck, they crowd your space, they talk trash behind your back, they offer nothing but a waste of time.

“I like to be left alone with my little family and that’s all I want. I’m just tweeting these to tell y’all to mind your manners,” he wrote.

“The fans don’t pay us. The company endorsements do,” Perry added in another tweet.

“The money is already on contract. My real fans know what I’m talking about and they watch out of love. The rest of y’all just salty.”
