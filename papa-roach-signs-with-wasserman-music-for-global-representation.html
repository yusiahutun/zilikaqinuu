Papa Roach Signs With Wasserman Music For Global Representation

August 2024 · 2 minute read

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Papa Roach has officially inked a deal with Wasserman Music for global representation, excluding Canada. The announcement was made by Jacoby Shaddix, who shared the band’s journey during the pandemic, expressing the belief in steering their destiny. The band’s focus on social media, their independent label, and assembling the right teams have created a unique niche.

Joining Wasserman Music’s diverse client roster, which includes renowned artists like Kenny Chesney, Coldplay, Billie Eilish, Kendrick Lamar, and more.

The two-time Grammy-nominated, Multi-Platinum-selling force in Alternative Hard Rock marked the 20th Anniversary of their groundbreaking album Infest and the iconic Last Resort in 2020. Since then, they’ve solidified their global presence, boasting 10 studio albums, including their recent release, Ego Trip, under New Noize Records. The album has garnered over 280 million global streams and produced three No. 1 singles, bringing their career total to 27 Top-10 hits and 11 No. 1s.

Recognized for championing mental health causes, Papa Roach is collaborating with the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention on their single “Leave A Light On (Talk Away The Dark),” which holds a Top-5 position at Active Rock and is #28 on the Alternative chart in the USA.

Wasserman Music, a leading global agency, represents various prominent artists across genres for live performances, touring, brand partnerships, and beyond. As long-term career development strategists, they collaborate with artists to expand their reach and impact globally. Wasserman Music, part of Wasserman, operates across 27 countries and over 62 cities, headquartered in Los Angeles, and plays a pivotal role in shaping culture and captivating audiences worldwide.
