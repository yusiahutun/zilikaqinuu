Brooke Kinsella is potential Celebrity Big Brother housemate

October 2024 · 2 minute read

The blonde former EastEnder is on a last-minute list for the new series that begins on Sunday.

Jailbird Boy George has already been forced to pull out due to the conditions of his probation.

And we can also reveal there are worries over US brothel queen Heidi Fleiss, also on probation, who may be forced to leave the house at any time to face drug tests by US officials.

Brooke, 26, whose brother Ben, 16, was stabbed to death outside a pub in North London in June 2008, has since devoted much of her time to campaigning.

And it is thought she may consider an approach to appear to help give her cause more publicity – and help save more lives.

An insider said: “She would consider it because it helps get her message out there.”

The need to sign up Brooke, who played troubled Kelly Taylor in EastEnders, is all the more urgent after fears emerged about Fleiss.

She is currently serving three years’ probation in Nevada after being busted for possession of the potent painkiller hydrocodone without a prescription.

One of the terms of that probation is that Fleiss, 43, submits to random drug tests – and US authorities say they will allow her to travel to Britain only if the condition is met.

Last night a pal of the former high-class Hollywood brothel owner declared: “She desperately wants to appear in Celebrity Big Brother.

“But they should know that British authorities might have to interrupt proceedings to take a blood sample to ensure she hasn’t been getting high.”

Another hellish headache for BB bosses could come from Ivana Trump, 60.

The Czech-born ex-wife of tycoon Donald Trump, who won a £15million divorce settlement in 1982, enjoys the high life and rarely travels without a huge entourage.

A former associate in America said yesterday: “Whenever she’s in London she stays in the same suite at The Dorchester.

“Heaven knows what she’ll make of roughing it in the BB house.

“They’ll either have to add some luxurious touches just for her or pay through the nose for her to agree to slum it.”

Others believed to have signed up are Ronnie Wood’s ex-lover Ekaterina Ivanova, 21, and pop singer Dane Bowers, 30.
