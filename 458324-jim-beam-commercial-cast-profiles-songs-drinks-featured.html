There's no doubt that Jim Beam has produced some of the best commercials. Talented men and women of influence have been featured in these adverts. So, who are Jim Beam commercial cast members?

Companies advertise their products to increase awareness. They use adverts in which celebrities are featured to endorse the products. Jim Beam commercial cast members have done an incredible job promoting the brand.

Jim Beam commercial cast

Jim Beam is an American brand of bourbon whiskey. It is produced in Clermont, Kentucky, by Beam Suntory. It was introduced in 1795 and is one of the best-selling brands of bourbon in the world.

The company has involved several celebrities in promoting Jim Beam. These stars have made sure that the customers know about the brand. Here are the actors and actresses and what you need to know about them.

Jordan features together with Adam Masters in the advert titled The Welcoming Spirit. He is the man in a jacket who is sweeping outside the bar. He walks around, insisting that people will always welcome you so long as there is Jim Beam.

Jordan James Smith is an actor, producer and film director. He has acted in New Girl, Her Boyfriend's Secret, and Bones.

David Ross

David appeared in a Jim Beam advert titled Tradition: New York Baseball. The ad begins with Ross posing as a bartender. The words “Rossy’s Fan Family” are inscribed on the screen.

David Ross is an American former professional baseball catcher. He is currently the manager of the Chicago Cubs of Major League Baseball. He was born on March 19th, 1977, in Bainbridge, Georgia, United States.

Mila Kunis

Who is the female bartender in the Jim Beam commercial? She is Mila Kunis. In the advert titled Something Different, she asks a patron whether he would like another beer. However, a couple nearby interjects to suggest an IPA, aged with their goat's milk.

Mila's latest Jim Beam commercial involved Jim Beam Bourbon. It is a short historic movie through time, looking at the brand's history through the ages.

Mila Kunis is an American actress. She was born on August 14th, 1983, in Chernivtsi, Ukraine. She is the daughter of Elvira, a physics teacher and Mark Kunis, a mechanical engineer.

Mila began acting in 1994 and has been active since. Over this period, she has acted in several movies. She has also won several awards.

Bartolo Colón

One of the funniest baseball players, Bartolo Colon, stars in a Jim Beam commercial titled Ball In Your Drink. He is a Dominican-American professional baseball pitcher. He has played for 11 Major League Baseball (MLB) teams, including Cleveland Indians and Chicago White Sox.

Bartolo Colón was born on May 24th, 1973, in Altamira, Dominican Republic. He made his MLB debut on April 4th, 1997, for the Cleveland Indians.
