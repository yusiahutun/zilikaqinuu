YouTube TV is an excellent live TV streaming service. There's no annual contract, channels to keep both adults and kids happy, and supports three streams and six accounts per household.

If you're thinking about signing up to YouTube TV, the most important consideration is what channels are available. That's why we've put together this handy list of all the channels on YouTube TV, along with their pricing.
