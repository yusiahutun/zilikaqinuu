Stephen Dorff is a well-known actor in Hollywood, famous for working in movies such as Blade (1998), Immortals (2011), Officer Down (2013), Heatstroke (2013), and I’ll Find You (2019).

Similarly, he has also appeared in many TV shows such as Deputy (2020), True Detective (2019), What a Dummy (1990 to 1991), and Star (2017 to 2018); Stephen has 90 credits in acting.

However, acting isn’t the only thing he is active in the show business; he has six credits as a producer and one as a writer. 

Stephen Dorff’s Dating Status

Stephen is not involved in the dating game right now. He has not been spotted or linked with anyone after his split with his last known girlfriend, Charlotte McKinney, in 2015.

Despite being out of the dating game, the actor has expressed his desire to get married and have kids. He said the following during an interview with The Guardian back in 2016:

I see myself getting married and having a family. I’d love to have a daughter. I don’t want to be one of those actors in my fifties who is alone.

Even though he doesn’t have his own kids, he does have a godchild. Stephen is a godfather to one of his friend’s babies.

Past Relationships

Stephen has dated a lot of women in the past. His public dating history begins in around 1993 when he was dating Milla Jovovich.

Then, in 1994, he dated the wonderful Reese Witherspoon. However, they broke up, and both of them moved on with their lives with Reese tying the knot with Ryan Phillipe. Now, Reese is married to Jim Toth.

In the same year, Stephen went out with Sports Illustrated and Victoria’s Secret model Bridget Hall.

Stephen made it three in a year, as he started going out with actress and model, Alicia Silverstone in 1994. They were together until some time around 1995.

Again, in 1995, Stephen got into a romantic tie with South African model Georgina Grenville. This tie lasted until 1996.

After Georgina, Stephen took a break from dating for almost two years before he finally got into a relationship with the amazing Jessica Alba. He started going out with her from 1998 and did so until their separation in 1999.

Then came another “break” before Jennifer Love-Hewitt came into Stephen’s life in 2003. The couple briefly went out but ended their companionship later that same year.

After that, Stephen involved himself with May Anderson for about a year: from some time around 2003 to 2004.

Another woman, Shannon Click, came into Stephen’s life in 2004. The two would go out for only a little while.

The third woman Stephen dated in 2004 is Pamela Anderson. They were spotted kissing a few times, and she was also seen leaving his apartment. However, this fling came to an end in around 2005.

After this, the Blade actor had short hookups with Sarah Harding and Rachel Stevens in 2006, Tami Donaldson from 2007 to 2008, and Leilani Dowding and Katharina Damm in 2012.
