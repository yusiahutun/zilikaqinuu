June 2024 · 3 minute read
  • Mzungu missionary Carey Joy and her husband, Bungoma boda boda rider Albert Wanyonyi, recently announced they were expecting their third child
  • Carey and Wanyonyi got married six years ago after meeting in Bungoma and she revealed communication was their main challenge as her man couldn't speak English
  • The humble Mzungu woman shared a video helping her husband build a house using mud, eliciting mixed reactions among netizens

Mzungu missionary Carey Joy is happily married to Bungoma boda boda rider, Albert Wanyonyi.

Is Mzungu Carey Joy suffering in marriage?

Carey shared a video helping her husband, Wanyonyi, build a house using mud, but a section of netizens were unimpressed.

The lovebirds, together with their two adorable kids, could be seen carrying mud and using it to build a small house, which Carey revealed was their newest ministry project.
