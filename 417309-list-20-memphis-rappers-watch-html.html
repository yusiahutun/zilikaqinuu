What is Memphis rap called?

The natives call it Memphis horrorcore or Memphis horrorcore hip-hop. Memphis locals initiated this regional subgenre of hip hop music in the early 1990s and gradually spread it worldwide.

Who started Memphis rap?

DJ Spanish Fly is the pioneer of Memphis hip-hop. He dropped out of high school in the 1980s to become an electro DJ. People filled up local clubs to capacity to listen to him playing Disco, Set it Off, R&B chart-toppers, and other famous rap hits.
