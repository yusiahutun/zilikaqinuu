13 Things Lizzie and Kathryn Fortunato Would Buy Again

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Emme Parsons Ernest Sandals

Emme Parsons Ernest Sandals

Credit: Emme Parsons

"I am eager for summer and to wear my favorite fisherman sandals. Emme Parsons really got these right—elegant but still durable, and not too utilitarian looking. I love these with a pair of jeans or a sarong and they’re making me ache for warmer weather." —Lizzie Fortunato

Caddis Life Wynton

Caddis Life Wynton

Credit: Caddis Life

"When I wear these, even men in the grocery compliment me! I wear the Wynton and though it took a little time to get used to their weight, they are now definitely a huge part of my look and personality. I also get comments on them!" —Kathryn Fortunato

Lisa Corti Cotton Cushion Cover 45x45cm

Lisa Corti Cotton Cushion Cover 45x45cm

Credit: Lisa Corti

"I’m a big fan of rearranging furniture and updating 'top layer' items when you need a home refresh and I did just that with these Lisa Corti pillow covers. They’re bright and loud and bold and wonderful, and I love shopping from a family-run brand that champions craftsmanship. Note: They also make a sarong in the matching fabric, so I'll be looking just as good as my bedroom this summer!" —LF

Exquisite J Ondina Socks

Exquisite J Ondina Socks

Credit: No.6 Store

"Fancy socks to wear with loafers in NYC's endless 'in between' weather (read: I want Spring so badly but it's still low 40's and my feet are freezing). I love the selection at one of my personal favorite boutiques, No. 6, which happens to be three blocks from our office so it's the perfect lunch-time-inspiration-pick-me-up!" —KF

Gigi Burris Isobel Hat

Gigi Burris Isobel Hat

Credit: Shopbop

"I gifted my husband a custom Gigi hat for Christmas but when we went to design it, I couldn’t help shopping for myself. I left with the summery, straw Isobel hat which looks ridiculously cute on and is easy to travel with (it’s already been to the Bahamas)." —LF

Fox Fodder Farm Medium Seasonal Signature Arrangement

Fox Fodder Farm Medium Seasonal Signature Arrangement

"Fresh flowers are my ultimate luxury. So I'd love to buy again-and again-and again (weekly?!) flower delivery from Fox Fodder Farm. We grew up with the founder, Taylor, and have been besties since age 5, so nothing makes me happier than supporting fellow girl boss creative entrepreneurs." —KF

Eolas Emily Leopard Cardigan

Eolas Emily Leopard Cardigan

"I recently discovered the new knitwear brand Eolas and fell in love with their joyful, vibrant pieces and their commitment to sustainability with their GRS-certified recycled cashmere. I've been living in their leopard print cardigan (very much a neutral in my book) but the lilac Mitch on my list for sprint (how cute over a swimsuit and jean shorts?)" —LF

Lizzie Fortunato Blossom Chain Necklace in Multi

Lizzie Fortunato Blossom Chain Necklace in Multi

Credit: Hampden Clothing

" I normally use our office as a rotating sample closet of jewelry to wear for an evening out or for the weekend, but I have to say that our Blossom Chain Necklace might be coming home with me for good. While I normally gravitate towards our beaded styles, there is something about the oversized gold-plated large link chain, the cluster of charms, and the front toggle closer that makes this my favorite current style. I've been pairing it with everything—even jeans and a sweatshirt to dress up a weekend playground outfit." —KF

Rescue Spa Facial with Soo Young Abrams

OJO Images//Getty Images

BOOK NOW

"I almost don't want to share this divine secret, but facials from Soo Young Abrams at Rescue Spa are the best indulgence. She is beyond cool and delivers a glow that I've never known from a facial before." —LF

Celsious Corewash Laundry Powder

Celsious Corewash Laundry Powder

"Lizzie and I just purchased the Corewash Laundry Powder and Brightener from sister-owned Williamsburg laundromat Celsious Social. In an effort to be more eco in everything we’re doing, I love that these products are non-toxic, biodegradable, and plastic-free. Plus, shopping small is core to Lizzie’s and my business and life values!" —KF

Lizzie Fortunato Fine Pink Topaz and Green Amethyst Duo Earrings

Lizzie Fortunato Fine Pink Topaz and Green Amethyst Duo Earrings

"These are my go-to earrings. They're handcrafted in 14K gold and are so lightweight I can easily wear them all day (and night), but they are still fun and poppy and way more exciting than a pair of diamond or pearl studs. If the pink's not for you, there's a range of available colors, but I think we all need a pop of pink in our lives." —LF

Porta NYC Palio Dinner Plate, The Panther

Porta NYC Palio Dinner Plate, The Panther

Credit: Porta NYC

"Our dear friend Francesca del Balzo recently opened Porta-NYC, the perfectly curated, Studio Giancarlo Valle-designed home goods store on Atlantic Avenue (which happens to be across from Outline Boutique—another new favorite). This shop is beautiful and if you don't live in New York, their website is equally inspiring. I love Porta's collection of hand-painted dinner plates originating from Sienna, Italy. I recently gifted two of the these dinner plates to our good friend Max Stein for his engagement. These plates are so great because once you get one or two, you'll be inspired to build a whole collection!" —KF

Sam Klemick Chair

Studio Sam Klemick

LEARN MORE

"I just finished a renovation and purchased the Bell Chair from the female, LA-based woodworker Sam Klemick. Her hand turned pieces are imaginative and bulbous and completely spark joy, and her studio salvages deadstock materials. My particular chair is upholstered in a vintage Kantha I brought back from a sourcing trip to India." –LF

Halie LeSavage is the fashion commerce editor at Harper's BAZAAR. Her style reporting covers everything from reviewing the best designer products to profiling emerging brands and designers. Previously, she was the founding retail writer at Morning Brew and a fashion associate at Glamour.
