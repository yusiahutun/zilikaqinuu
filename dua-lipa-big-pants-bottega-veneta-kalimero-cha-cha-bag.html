Dua Lipa Wears Big Top and Pants With Bottega Veneta's Party Bag

April 2024 · 2 minute read

If the fashion It girls of today have taught us anything, it’s that contrasts are in: big pants and tiny tops, oversize blazers and miniskirts, little black dresses and chunky sneakers. The list goes on. This weekend, Dua Lipa stepped out in two casually cool looks full of contrasts.

TheImageDirect.com / ipa-agency.net

On Saturday, the “Houdini” singer emerged in New York City wearing Bottega Veneta’s boxy black wool twill top with big sleeves and a high mock neck that can be buttoned up or down. She paired the oversize top with big gray jeans and gold-frame shades with dark lenses by Supreme Elm, plus Bottega Veneta’s ultimate party bag: the Kalimero Cha-Cha Bag. The woven calfskin leather mini features a long shoulder strap and brass hardware; it comes in inky black, mustard brown, and a bright tulip red. You can purchase your own for a cool $3,400.

Bottega Veneta Kalimero Cha-Cha Bag

Bottega Veneta Kalimero Cha-Cha Bag

For what looked like a low-key day of errands, the pop star seemed to wear no makeup and had her fiery red hair held up with a black clip.

TheImageDirect.com / ipa-agency.net

The songwriter pulled out the Cha-Cha again on Sunday, for a day out in the city with boyfriend Callum Turner. She styled it then with a black blazerlike top with long sleeves and hidden buttons down the middle, light-wash mom jeans, and brown tortoiseshell oval sunglasses with gold accents on the sides. Lipa had her hair pulled back into a messy ponytail broken up into three braids. She wore a glossy lip and a grungy black manicure.
