Gino D'Acampo says his wife isn't jealous of him being surrounded by hundreds of women

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Gino D'Acampo has lifted the lid on his marriage to his wife Jessica Stellina Morrison, admitting she isn't "jealous" of him being surrounded by hundreds of women.

The This Morning chef married Jessica in 2002, and revealed the secret to their long-lasting marriage is down to trust.

Speaking to the Mail's Weekend magazine, he said: "I employ more than 700 women in my restaurants. In TV, I'm surrounded by women. I love it.

"Women are more interesting than men, but my wife loves it too. She doesn't get jealous.

"She understands that if you want to be unfaithful, you'll do it anyway. It's a matter of trust."
