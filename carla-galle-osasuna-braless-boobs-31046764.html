Model who went to footy game braless hits out for 'making her boobs the only topic'

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Spanish model and actress Clara Galle went viral in the week after she went braless to a football game, and she’s since hit out at fans for ‘making her boobs the only topic of the game’.

Galle, 21, attended Osasuna’s La Liga home clash against Sevilla on Saturday, but her appearance as a guest of her local club caused a stir amongst supporters. Galle, who has 4million followers on Instagram, is most famous for her role as Raquel in Through My Window.

Born in Pamplona, Galle is an Osasuna fan and revealed she’s being going to watch games at El Sadar since she was a child. She was snapped walking around the ground and was pitch side before kick-off, with Osasuna posting the pictures to their various social media pages.
