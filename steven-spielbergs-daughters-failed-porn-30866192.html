She confessed that she had gone on a “healing journey” with the “life-affirming” work after years of struggling with her mental health and denied that her parents had any shame.

"I was heartbroken, vulnerable, and felt like my soul had kind of been split in two. And I was covering that up with drinking... I am no longer like that."

Dad ‘embarrassed’

Though Mikaela insisted she won’t do “hardcore content” reports emerged at the time that her famous director dad wasn’t happy with what she was putting out into the world.

Going under the moniker ‘Sugar Star’, “sexual creature” Mikaela self-produces porn and fetish videos on the internet.

And not long after her decision went public, a family friend claimed to Page Six: “Outwardly, Steven and Kate — who are the most evolved parents in the world and love their kids beyond all measure — have always been supportive of Mikaela and try to understand her.

“Obviously, though, they’re embarrassed by her sudden public admission of entry into the sex worker world."

The source added: “Nothing can hurt Steven Spielberg’s immaculate reputation, nor Kate’s, but they’re worried about how it looks for their other kids, who are trying to be supportive but who are also embarrassed.

“This certainly isn’t how they were raised.”

But for Mikaela, she insists that her parents were “expecting” her to do a “taboo art”.

She told The Sun: “My parents, despite what people have written, aren’t embarrassed. They were actually kind of expecting it to be my career… some sort of outsider art… or taboo art, because they’ve always known that I was a little bit neurotic.”

Age-gap romance

Mikaela’s chosen career path wasn’t the only thing that raised eyebrows – so did her age-gap romance with her 54-year-old fiancé, Chuck Pankow.

Mikaela is 27, which makes Chuck 27 years older than her.

She has described him as her “rock” and Chuck says he is “fine” with Mikaela’s decision to venture into the adult film industry.

He confessed: “It was a little weird at first when she was telling me what she wanted to do - I thought it was a little unusual.

“A conversation we had, I asked her, 'But I feel you have so much more to offer the world than t*ts and a** and she looked at me and said 'Well why not both?’ So I couldn't really argue with that.”

Arrest

It was after an incident involving Chuck that Mikaela was arrested in February 2020, when she was 23.

The star was admitted into the Hill Detention Centre in Nashville, Tennessee, on a misdemeanour of domestic violence, allegedly against her fiancé.

Bail of $1,000 was posted for the star and she was released after a 12-hour hold behind bars.

Her arrest warrant claimed she was at a bar with Chuck when the pair got into an argument, and Mikaela allegedly began throwing objects at him – one of which hit his hand and caused an injury.

Eventually, however, the case was dropped in September 2020, after Mikaela completed court-ordered counselling sessions.
