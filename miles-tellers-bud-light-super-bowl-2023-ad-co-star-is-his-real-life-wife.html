Miles Teller's Bud Light Super Bowl 2023 Ad Co-Star Is His Real-Life Wife

March 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Miles Teller and Keleigh Sperry Teller are acting together for a Bud Light Super Bowl ad. People reported the news on February 2 and wrote that the commercial will be shown on February 12 during Super Bowl LVII. Miles, Keleigh, and their French bulldog Bugsy are featured in the commercial, in which Miles gets himself and his wife Bud Lights while on hold waiting for a customer service representative. The two then dance as the hold music plays and they wait for someone to come on the line. 

Teller opened up to People about the positive experience. "It's really been an incredible year for both Keleigh and I, not just professionally but personally," Miles said. "So, when Bud Light came and wanted us to be a part of a Super Bowl commercial, I mean, that's the top of the mountain as far as these things go. We always look forward to watching them." He also took to Twitter to post the commercial on February 2. "If you're stuck on hold, you might as well make it #EasyToEnjoy," Miles wrote. "Who's got their @Budlight ready for the Super Bowl? We'll see you at commercial break #sponsored." 

But this isn't the first time Miles and Keleigh have collaborated. The two also starred in Taylor Swift's music video for "I Bet You Think About Me (Taylor's Version)" in November 2021. And, of course, the two have shared special moments together as a couple on social media.
