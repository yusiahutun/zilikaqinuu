Daisy Edgar-Jones is the epitome of chic in a black lace gown as she attends the LACMA Art+Film Gala

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Daisy Edgar-Jones was the epitome of chic as she attended the 2023 LACMA Art+Film Gala, presented by Gucci at Los Angeles County Museum of Art on Saturday.

The actress, 25, looked sensational in a black figure-hugging lace gown, with a daring thigh-high split detail.

She elevated her height in matching strappy heels and accessorised with dainty silver jewellery. 

Daisy attended the glitzy event alongside A-list talents including Jennifer Lopez , Kim Kardashian, Heidi Klum and Salma Hayek, among many others.

LACMA trustee Eva Chow and actor Leonardo DiCaprio co-chaired the event for the twelfth consecutive year.

Notables from the art, film, fashion, and entertainment industries gathered at LACMA for its twelfth annual Art+Film Gala honoring artist Judy Baca.

Proceeds from the annual Art+Film Gala go toward underwriting LACMA’s initiative to make film more central to the museum’s curatorial programming, while also funding LACMA’s broader mission.
