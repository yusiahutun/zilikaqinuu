Meaning of (Farewell, Neverland) by TOMORROW X TOGETHER

April 2024 · 3 minute read

The TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT) song titled '네버랜드를 떠나며 (Farewell, Neverland)' presents a bittersweet farewell to their fictional world, Neverland, where the members have found solace and comfort. The overall theme of the song revolves around leaving behind a familiar place and embarking on a new journey, even if it is filled with uncertainty.One standout lyric that captures the essence of the song is "Neverland, my love, 이젠 안녕" (Neverland, my love, 이젠 안녕). This line symbolizes the appreciation and love the members have for Neverland, a place where they felt safe and understood. However, it also represents their readiness to let go and move on. This theme of bidding farewell to a familiar place is relatable to anyone who has experienced leaving something behind, be it a physical location or an emotional state.Another significant lyric is "별들아 모두 편히 자렴 'Til I be calling" (Stars, rest in peace until I be calling). This line expresses a yearning for peace and tranquility, suggesting that even when leaving behind something familiar, there is a desire for a sense of calmness and serenity. It can be seen as a reflection of the members' longing for peace within themselves as they face the uncertainties of the future.The phrase "여긴 no home" (Here is no home) emphasizes the feeling of displacement and not belonging. This lyric delves into the theme of searching for a place where one truly feels a sense of home. It conveys the idea that leaving behind a familiar place can create a sense of uneasiness and a need to find a new sense of belonging. The members acknowledge the fear that comes with this journey but express their determination to explore and discover further.The line "아름다웠던 그 모든 게 진실이 아니란 것을 알기에 난" (Knowing that everything beautiful wasn't the truth) explores the theme of disillusionment and coming to terms with the reality of a situation. It suggests that even in the utopia they have experienced, there were elements of falsehood and deception, reminding listeners that appearances can be deceiving. This theme reminds us to question our perceptions and confront the truth, even if it is uncomfortable.Overall, the song '네버랜드를 떠나며 (Farewell, Neverland)' touches on various themes such as bidding farewell, seeking peace, searching for belonging, and reconciling with the truth. The lyrics amplify these themes and provide a window into the members' emotions as they embark on a new journey. Through the lens of their farewell to Neverland, TXT invites listeners to reflect on their own experiences with leaving behind familiar places and the emotions that arise from such transitions.
