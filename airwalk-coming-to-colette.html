90s Skater Girl Style is Staging a Comeback

September 2024 · 1 minute read

It seems we've mined nearly every '90s trend, from stark minimalism to chokers and Clueless style plaids—but the skater girl has yet to reclaim her moment in the (most likely California) sunshine, until now. Airwalk shoes, the kind worn by Chloe Sevigny during her "Sassy" days and long-haired grunge-era boys are staging a comeback by way of Paris. The seminal '90s brand is just debuted at Colette, complete with a full window installation. With the rise of Supreme and Vetements streetwear, now seems like the perfect time to let skater style have its due—skateboard not required.

Kerry Pieri is Harper's Bazaar's Digital Fashion/Features Director.
