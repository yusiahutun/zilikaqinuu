Jose Chameleone's Wife Sends Him Powerful, Encouraging Words as He Battles Sickness

  • Daniella, who lives in the USA, has proved to be a caring and loving wife to Chameleone, despite their differences
  • Fans of the singer have praised Daniella for being such a great wife

Joseph Mayanja, popularly known as Jose Chameleone, is still undergoing treatment at Nakasero Hospital in Kampala, Uganda.

The music giant's wife, Daniella Atim, revealed that he was diagnosed with H.pylori, but according to some Ugandan media outlets, he is battling liver and pancreas related disorders.

Daniella, who lives in the USA, has proved to be a caring and loving wife to Chameleone, despite their differences.

The mother of five has sent powerful messages to her husband and those going through difficult times, assuring them that God is with them.

She shared a series of biblical, talking about the goodness of the Lord.

"God restores me to health and heals my wounds, " she said quoting Jeremiah 30:176.

Daniella shared another verse, and she said:

" God will provide everything I need."

Fans of the father of six have sent prayers and positive vibes his way, hoping to see him back on foot, doing what he loves most.

Chameleone's wife protects their children

Photos of the father of six, looking emaciated, lying on a hospital bed, have been making rounds on social media.

Daniella has lashed out at those sharing photos of Chameleone, telling them to respect her family. The protective mother told people to stop sharing Chameleone's photos because they might affect his kids.

Daniella said it is so 'unpleasant' for people to share such photos of Chameleone, whose health isn't good.

"Why are we all sharing that so 'unpleasant photo of our personal person'? Do you guys realise he has children far from him whose emotional well being you and I should protect?" she posed.

Daniella said that despite trying to calm her kids down not to panic about their dad's health status, disrespectful people were still sharing photos.

"So am busy looking collected explaining to my children that pilori is a bacterial infection that should clear with consistent treatment, and you are all sharing a damn picture. Don’t we all have a happy picture of our favourite human?"
