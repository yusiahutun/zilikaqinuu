Reggie Couz Bio and Age

Reggie Couz Musical career

What prepared him for his musical career was his education at the Cicely Tyson School of Performing and Fine Arts in East Orange. There he had the opportunity to learn classical music in several languages, including Spanish, German, Italian, and Russian.

He then went to the University of Arts in Philadelphia, where he decided to study music but could not stay there for more than a year.

Thanks to this musical background and his skills, Reggie was able to develop the character of Mr. Johnson’s hip-hop choir, which has become very popular on YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram. He has also released a number of songs including Overnight, Holy Tony, Goin Down, and M.O.H.

Even though his music hasn’t become what you might call mainstream, he still managed to get his fair share of his fan base, which consists mainly of people from his social media circle. The biggest part of the listeners he gets are therefore mainly people from YouTube. Nevertheless, his songs are also on various other platforms like SoundCloud, where he has over 55 thousand followers, iTunes, Tidal, and many others.
