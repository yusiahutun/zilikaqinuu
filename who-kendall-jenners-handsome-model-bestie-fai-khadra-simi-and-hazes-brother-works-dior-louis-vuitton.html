Fai Khadra was born to a Palestinian family in Los Angeles

Per GQ Middle East, Fai was born in Los Angeles to Palestinian parents but has lived in cities including London and Riyadh.

Although he has a multicultural background, he resolutely defines himself Palestinian. “Whenever people ask me where I’m from, I always say I’m Palestinian,” he told the publication.

According to Grazia, Fai comes from an artistic family. His dad is an art collector, while his mum runs a women’s fashion boutique called The Art of Living in Saudi Arabia. It is perhaps not too surprising then to hear that Fai himself is a trained architect, as stated in his interview with GQ.

His sisters are Simi and Haze Khadra

Fai is the brother of 30-year-old identical twins Simi and Haze Khadra, known for being DJs, models and beauty entrepreneurs. According to Elle, Simi and Haze – aka Simihaze on socials – have been regulars at fashion weeks since their early teens.

He’s a model

Khadra has fronted campaigns for everyone from Ralph Lauren and Zara to Bulgari, Moncler, Louis Vuitton and Alexander Wang.

In October, he walked Balenciaga’s spring-summer 2024 runway in Paris, per GQ. He also joined fellow celebs Jenna Ortega, Stranger Things’ Joseph Quinn and Maya Hawke, The Woman King’s Thuso Mbedu, singer Liu Yuxin and French rapper Orelsan to front a new campaign for Dior Beauty’s Gris Dior.

He was announced as an ambassador for the French brand in April. “I think that the most common values that I share with the brand are a visual and design appreciation, a love for fashion and most importantly a love for art,” he told GQ.

He’s a songwriter

Khadra also unleashes his creativity in songwriting. According to Grazia, he has written songs for musicians such as Justine Skye and Blood Orange and also posts his own work on SoundCloud.

He has A-list friends

Khadra is already known for being one of Kendall Jenner’s closest confidantes – he’s also hung out with her family and made cameos on Keeping Up with the Kardashians – but he counts plenty of other A-listers among his inner circle too.

He’s been spotted with celebs like Gigi and Bella Hadid, Hailey Bieber, Leonardo DiCaprio’s girlfriend Vittoria Ceretti, Rita Ora, Jordyn Woods and Kendall’s sister Kylie Jenner. He’s been linked to so many model friends that Elle even dubbed him “the model set’s favourite man friend”.

Who has he dated?

Initially, there were rumours that Khadra and Kendall Jenner were dating, after they were seen together on countless occasions, but the Victoria’s Secret model put that speculation to rest in 2019 when she posted a selfie of them on Instagram with the caption: “We don’t date he’s just my date.”
