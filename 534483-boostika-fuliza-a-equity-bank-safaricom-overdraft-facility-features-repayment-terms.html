Boostika vs Fuliza: A Look Into Equity Bank, Safaricom Overdraft Facility Features, Repayment Terms

February 2024 Â· 3 minute read
  • Equity Bank Kenya launched the Boostika overdraft facility, which allows customers to complete transactions on low account balance
  • The facility took on Safaricom Fuliza M-Pesa product, which is already available in the market
  • Although the two credit facilities serve similar purposes, they differ in terms of payments, limits and other features

TUKO.co.ke journalist Wycliffe Musalia brings over five years of experience in financial, business, and technology reporting, offering deep insights into Kenyan and global economic trends.

Equity Bank Kenya has unveiled an overdraft facility similar to the Safaricom M-Pesa Fuliza product.

The credit facilities serve the same purpose, allowing customers to complete their transactions in case they run into insufficient account balances.

Equity Bank's overdraft facility, dubbed Boostika, gives customers a minimum limit of KSh 100 and a maximum of KSh 100,000.

In an exclusive interview with TUKO.co.ke, Equity Bank noted that the limit is awarded based on account activities and customers' credit scores.

"The limit is awarded based entirely on your account profile. Various parameters include how frequently you operate your Equity bank account in a month, the average amount that remains in the account, how well you pay your loans if you have ever borrowed with us, and if you have ever defaulted on a loan in any financial institution, among others," said Equity Bank customer care.

Who is eligible for Equity Bank Boostika?

Boostika can be accessed by Equity Bank customers sending money to mobile wallets or a Bank account.

The service is only available for payments of goods and services through the One Equity Till number or buying airtime.

Once your account runs low, you get a prompt message asking you to accept Boostika and the payments are made through USSD 247#, Equitel or Equity Mobile App.

"Boostika is a convenient loan, especially during such a time when parents and guardians are taking their children to school. I have just used the facility to top up my payment for the school uniforms," Eric, a parent based in Kangemi, Westlands told TUKO.co.ke.

When can one access Boostka and Fuliza limits?

On the other hand, the Fuliza overdraft facility is available to Safaricom M-Pesa customers.

Fuliza is available to every registered M-Pesa line, treated as a different account.

Just like Boostika, the Fuliza subscribers must be active users of M-Pesa for a period of six months and above.

Both facilities require a good credit score to boost the limit.

For Boostika, the limit is reviewed on a monthly basis, based on the repayment history.

Fuliza limits are reviewed every three months based on the frequency of M-Pesa service usage and range up to KSh 70,000.

Fuliza, Boostika repayment terms

Equity Bank Boostika has a repayment period of up to 30 days, and the amount due is repaid through an Auto Sweep from the borrower's operative account.

Boostika has a processing Fee (LACE) of 5%, Insurance of 1%, excise duty of 20% and an interest of 18 % per annum.

Fuliza M-Pesa is repaid within 14 days and has a daily maintenance fee depending on the amount borrowed, an excise duty of 20% and a tariff fee.

A customer who fails to pay after 30 days will not have access to the Fuliza M-PESA limit.

Where is Fuliza, Boostika used?

  • Boostika is only used to complete transactions when you run low on funds when making a payment, sending money, or buying airtime.
  • Fuliza is used to make payments for goods and services, buy airtime and send money when your account runs low on cash.
  • With Fuliza, you can also withdraw the money - based on your limit - at the M-Pesa agent.
