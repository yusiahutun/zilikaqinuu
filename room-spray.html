The best room sprays for enhancing your home

March 2024 · 6 minute read

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

1

Earl of East Greenhouse Home Mist

1

Earl of East Greenhouse Home Mist

Credit: Cult Beauty

Earl of East’s home fragrances are batch-blended in Hackney and packaged using UK-made amber glass. Not just a low-impact option, they smell incredible too: our pick is Greenhouse, which enlivens a kitchen or living room with piercingly green tomato leaf and verdant herbs.

2

Trudon Abd el Kader Room Spray

2

Trudon Abd el Kader Room Spray

Credit: Harrods

Staying true to tradition, Cire Trudon’s room sprays arrive in green fluted glass bottles hand-crafted in Tuscany. Inspired by the heat of North Africa, the sublime Abd el Kader fragrance blends spiced tea with juicy top notes of blackcurrant and mint.

3

Diptyque 34 Boulevard Saint Germain Room Spray

3

Diptyque 34 Boulevard Saint Germain Room Spray

Credit: Diptyque

Diptyque’s most sentimental scent pays homage to the brand’s original Parisian boutique, which first opened its doors in the 1960s. Complex and sophisticated, it features fresh, green notes of blackcurrant leaf and fig over a gentle base of blonde woods. Sweet, creamy, and instantly comforting.

4

Ilapothecary Beat the Blues Room Spray

4

Ilapothecary Beat the Blues Room Spray

Credit: Cult Beauty

For a fragrance that truly alters your mood, look to Ilaapothecary’s spookily effective Beat The Blues blend. The pure blend of geranium, petitgrain and tuberose essential oils will transform your outlook in a few deep breaths. This is a highly potent aroma, so spray with restraint to enjoy it at its best – and remember to breathe slowly and deeply. It's perfect for setting the scene before a yoga session, too.

5

Floris London Hyacinth and Bluebell Room Fragrance

5

Floris London Hyacinth and Bluebell Room Fragrance

Credit: Liberty London

Queen Elizabeth II famously wore Floris' White Rose perfume, and so the brand became the first perfumery she personally bestowed a Royal Warrant upon.

The brand's room sprays are every bit as refined as its famous fragrances: Hyacinth and Bluebell is a complex combination of feather-light florals, recreating the multifaceted aroma of an abundant bouquet of flowers.

6

This Works Deep Sleep Pillow Spray

6

This Works Deep Sleep Pillow Spray

Credit: Liberty London

This Works has used science to explore the soporific properties of its 'functional fragrance' Deep Sleep line – and the results are impressive. Neuroscientist-led clinical trials have found the aromatherapy blend is able to activate areas of the brain associated with pleasure and calmness, paving the way for productive rest. This fan-favourite pillow spray can also be spritzed on pyjamas and around your room to maximise its benefits.

7

Frédéric Malle 1er Mai Perfume Gun

7

Frédéric Malle 1er Mai Perfume Gun

Credit: Liberty London

One for the serious fragrance lovers, Frédéric Malle’s ‘perfume gun’ comes in a host of complex, accomplished scents. Created by celebrated nose and long-time Malle collaborator, Dominique Ropion, 1er Mai nods to French tradition with a bright, positive note of lily of the valley: the flower traditionally gifted to loved ones on the first of May.

8

Loewe Home Fragrance - Tomato Leaves

8

Loewe Home Fragrance - Tomato Leaves

Credit: Net a porter

Loewe’s fragrance line sits at the centre of the designer homeware boom, and there’s a scent in the gardening-inspired line to please everyone. Each of the 11 blends smells remarkably like the real thing: Tomato Leaves is green and prickly, while Juniper Berry is peppery and bracing.

9

Jo Malone London Lime Basil and Mandarin Room Spray

9

Jo Malone London Lime Basil and Mandarin Room Spray

Credit: Lookfantastic

Plenty of Jo Malone London’s scents lend themselves well to the home, but for the arrival of spring it has to be the legendary Lime, Basil and Mandarin. The fresh, herb-spiked citrus will instantly energise any room.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Aromatherapy Associates Deep Relax Sleep Mist

10

Aromatherapy Associates Deep Relax Sleep Mist

Credit: Sephora

Aromatherapy Associates' classic Deep Relax collection makes a thoughtful gift for anyone going through an anxious time. It’s deeper and earthier than you’re likely expecting, eschewing the usual lavender in favour of calming chamomile and sandalwood. Try spritzing it on your bedding, too.

11

Aesop Olous Aromatique Room Spray

11

Aesop Olous Aromatique Room Spray

Credit: Net a porter

Those who don't favour traditional florals should turn to Aesop: the brand makes some of the most unique, transportive home fragrances, from candles to incense and potent room sprays.

Speaking to the notion of 'forest bathing', the brand's Olous aroma is at once green and woody, conjuring the calming properties of an oak-filled forest with a single spritz.

12

Seed to Skin Borgo Air Room Spray

12

Seed to Skin Borgo Air Room Spray

Credit: Liberty London

Jeanette Throttrup, founder of the revered Borgo Santo Pietro retreat, has bottled up the bracing scent of her idyllic Tuscan estate in this sublime room spray. Blooming roses mingle with botanical herbs and fresh, green leaves. Expect serious olfactory bliss.

13

Neals Yard Remedies Organic Aromatherapy Room Spray

13

Neals Yard Remedies Organic Aromatherapy Room Spray

Credit: Neals Yard Remedies

For something zesty and uplifting, this organic essential-oil blend from Neals Yard Remedies fits the bill perfectly. With smooth petitgrain and zingy grapefruit, it's one for bright spring mornings.

14

Perfumer H Salt Room Spray

14

Perfumer H Salt Room Spray

Inventive and unique, Lyn Harris' Perfumer H recreates the scents of nature with a surreal slant. Salt conjures the idea of warm earth with a blend of thyme, cedar and comforting angelica, with a hint of frankincense for warmth.

15

The White Company Grapefruit & Mandarin Home Spray

15

The White Company Grapefruit & Mandarin Home Spray

Credit: The White Company

The White Company makes a collection of beautiful room sprays, and while Seychelles and Pomegranate are the fan favourites, we're currently reaching for the zesty Grapefruit and Mandarin. Sunny and sparkling, with a hint of amber for depth, it's especially well-placed in a kitchen.

16

Susanne Kaufmann Calming Pillow Spray

16

Susanne Kaufmann Calming Pillow Spray

Credit: Cult Beauty

Born in Susanne Kaufmann's famously serene Alpine spa, this soothing pillow spray can be spritzed over sheets to slow a racing mind before bed. It's a modern, refreshing take on lavender, with bright lime and orange to stop any sense of stuffiness.

17

Urban Apothecary Oudh Geranium Luxury Room Spray

17

Urban Apothecary Oudh Geranium Luxury Room Spray

Credit: Sephora

Rich oud scents can make the most high-ceilinged rooms feel cosy and inviting. Urban Apothecary's potent offering blends incense-like spice with the rosy comfort of geranium to create a warm and woody blend that's perfect for cold nights.

18

Balance Room Spray Cowshed

18

Balance Room Spray Cowshed

Credit: Lookfantastic

A spritz of this room spray will make any space feel as calm and inviting as Cowshed's famous spas. It's a deep, enriching scent with rose geranium and frankincense: a blend that's sure to lift spirits on gloomy days.

19

Fig Trees Jo Loves

19

Fig Trees Jo Loves

Credit: Jo Loves

Fig is a perfect fragrance for the home: the green and creamy note feels comforting yet clean. Trust Jo Malone to harness it beautifully: this room spray uses sweet cassis to bolster the joy-inducing aroma.

20

Dr Vranjes Firenze Peonia Black Jasmine Room Spray

20

Dr Vranjes Firenze Peonia Black Jasmine Room Spray

Credit: Liberty London

Floral lovers will adore Dr Vranjes' most intoxicating home scent. Made in Tuscany, Peonia Black Jasmine is a bold ode to white florals, with a suede-like base of moss to ground the rich, sweet notes.
