Gurlez Akhtar is the punjabi known singer who is known for her singing skills in superhit punjabi films like Jawani Zindabaad (2010), Hashar (2008) and Guddiyan Patole (2019). Not only she worked in films but she also she have collaborated with many famous Punjabi singers like R.Nait, Garry Sandhu and many more. She have released many of her solo songs too like Meri Wait, parche and many more.

Career

She started her singing career by singing a duet with Sukhwinder Sukhi from his album “Sharif Munda” in 2004. She was in VIIII class when she sung her first song. After then, She has given her voice in Punjabi movies like, ‘Hashar (2008), ‘Jihne Mera Dil Luteya (2011),’ ‘Ardaas (2016),’ and many more.
