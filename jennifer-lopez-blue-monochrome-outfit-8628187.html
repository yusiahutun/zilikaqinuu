See Jennifer Lopez's All-Blue Outfit

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Monochrome mania has officially reached an all-time high. It's almost impossible to walk down the street without seeing someone wearing a tone-on-tone look — whether it's a sweatsuit or an actual pantsuit. And Jennifer Lopez is the latest celebrity to put her signature stamp on the trend.

On Sunday, J.Lo stepped out for a sunny stroll in New York City, dressed in head-to-toe blue — but rather than sticking to a singular shade, she kept her outfit interesting with several different hues. On top, the "On the Floor" singer wore a navy blue turtleneck underneath a matching wool coat that hit below the knee, and, on bottom, she went much lighter with stonewash jeans and baby blue suede stiletto boots. She kept the blue theme going with her accessories, adding a bright blue quilted Chanel handbag and blue-tinted aviator sunglasses to the mix. 

Gold hoops, a glossy dark pink lip, and slicked-back bun provided the finishing touches to her street style ensemble. 

The evening before, J.Lo wore a nearly-identical pair of XXL jeans for her girls' night out with film producer Elaine Goldsmith-Thomas. Going the cozy-chic route with her outfit on that occasion, Lopez teamed her baggy, high-waisted denim, which featured frayed hems and huge pockets, with a chunky, cropped turtleneck sweater in a cream color. She accessorized with tall beige platform heels, a croc-embossed Birkin bag, another pair of aviators, and a gold bracelet and earrings.
