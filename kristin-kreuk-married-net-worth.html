Kristin Kreuk is a Canadian actress who gained popularity for her appearance as Lana Lang in the superhero TV series, Smallville.

Does Kristin Kreuk have a Sister?

Kristin opened her eyes on 30th December 1982 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada as Kristin Laura Kreuk. Her sun sign is Capricorn.  The name of her parents is Peter Kreuk (Father) and Deanna Che (Mother).

Besides that, she has a sister whose name is Justine Kreuk. From a young age, she was interested in acting. Her nationality is Canadian and she belongs to the white ethnicity.

Talking about her education, she completed her graduation from Eric Hamber High School in Vancouver. Besides that, she has done a few stage work in school.
