Adelaine Morin Bio, Family, Boyfriend, Salary and Net Worth

Adelaine Morin is a Canadian YouTube star famous for her fashion blogs. Her self-named channel is filled with her make-up instructional videos, shopping pulls, skin and hair care schedule, and so on. Moreover, Morin has over 2.4 million subscribers on her channel. 

Similarly, she made her second YouTube channel named Adelaines Camera Roll, where she posts all unboxing and travel blogs. Additionally, she is the winner of Seventeen magazine’s Beauty Smartie Showdown in 2012.
