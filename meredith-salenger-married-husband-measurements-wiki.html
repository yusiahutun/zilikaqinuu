Meredith Salenger Wiki

Meredith Dawn Salenger was born on 14 March 1970 in Malibu, California, USA, to a Russian-Jewish family. Her mother Dorothy is an interior designer, her father Gary Salenger is a dentist. Salenger was born a natural actor. As a child, she often appeared in front of family and friends. Her mother supported her talent and registered her for acting lessons at the age of 8.

She quickly started her acting career and soon became a child star who made her debut in the feature Annie. Besides acting Salenger also won a number of TV commercials.

Salenger became a star after he played the title role in the movie “The Journey of Natty Gann” in 1985. For her great performance, she received the Young Artist Award for Best Actress. Before he turned 18, Salenger had appeared in about 5 movies, including A Night in the Life of Jimmy Reardon.

Salenger’s acting career grew rapidly, but she tried to pursue a different passion. She left Hollywood at the age of 18 and enrolled in Harvard, where she graduated cum laude in psychology in 1992.

She also attended the Straus Institute for Dispute Resolution at Pepperdine University School of Law, where she received Court-Based Mediation of Family Law Matters in 2009 and Mediating the Litigated Case in 2011.
