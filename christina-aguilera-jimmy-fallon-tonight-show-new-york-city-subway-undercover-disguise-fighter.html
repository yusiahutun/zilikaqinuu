Christina Aguilera Wows NYC Subway While Singing Undercover With Jimmy Fallon

Christina Aguilera probably didn't fool anybody when she went undercover to busk in the New York City subway with Jimmy Fallon on "The Tonight Show" Thursday.

A hat, wig and sunglasses couldn't cover up Aguilera's most recognizable feature -- her voice. We can't imagine onlookers weren't immediately suspicious when they began gathering around the singer belting out Aretha Franklin's hit "Think."
