June Holidays and Observances in 2023

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Ah, June. One of our favorite months on the calendar! It's full of high-profile special days, like Father's Day and Juneteenth, but it is also filled with other fun observances (Leave the Office Early Day? Say less). Named after Juno, Roman goddess of marriage, home, and family, June is a fun-filled month with kids out of school and everyone in vacation mode.

From the official first day of summer and the longest day of the year to backyard barbecues and road trips to the coast, June gives us endless reasons to let loose and have some fun.
