Once you are a fan of the long-running web series Eastside or even Fresh Off the Boat, you will easily be able to establish a relationship with Constance Wu. The woman is an American actress who, although she has not been in Hollywood forever, has become one of the most influential people in the world.

Constance Wu Biography (Age)

March 22, 1982, was the day Constance Wu was born in Richmond, Virginia. Her birth name is Constance Tianming Wu, and her parents were originally from Taiwan before she decided to settle in the United States. In Richmond, where she was born, Constance was raised by her father, a genetics professor, and her mother, a computer programmer, along with her three other sisters, two older and one younger sister.

For her education, the very interesting and influential actress attended Douglas S. Freeman High School, from where she transferred to the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute to obtain a certificate that would improve her acting skills. In 2005, she graduated from the State University of New York at Purchase, where she earned a Fine Arts degree in Acting. She also went to Columbia University, where she again earned a degree in speech pathology.

Since she was a young girl, Wu always wanted to be an actress. As a result, she went to a community theater at the age of 12. She nailed her first acting role in the theater with a role in a play, Wind in the Willows. When she went to high school and even beyond, she continued to act in more theater productions. While still a student, she looked for jobs in theaters that would help her learn and get used to the life of a professional actor.

Constance Wu Career 

Already at a very young age, the actress began to read many books about acting and she never got tired to practice what she read. With this in mind, her talent was solidly built and it helped her secure her first movie role in The Sound of my Voice after she moved from New York, where she had studied, to Los Angeles, where she embarked on an acting career.

Soon she would appear in other films such as Deadly Vengeance, Television with the Red Chinese, Best Friends Forever, and finally The Feels and Crazy Rich Asians. Among the things she has worked on TV are Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Covert Affairs, and Fresh Off the Boat, which remains one of her best performances. She also appeared in the successful web series EastSiders.

Accolades

Constance Wu has received several award nominations for her numerous productions. She has been nominated twice for the Indie Series Award, the Gold Derby Awards, and a nomination for the Critics’ Choice Television Award as best actress in a comedy series. The woman was also nominated for the TCA Award of the TV Critics Association TCA for individual achievement in comedy. She has received a total of 10 nominations for her name so far.

For her real followers, the actress will soon appear in the film Crazy Rich Asians, which will be released on the big screen towards the end of 2018.
