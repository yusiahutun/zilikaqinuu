Who is Michael Rapaport?

Michael Rapaport is a renowned American actor, director, producer, writer, and comedian. He was born Michael David Rapaport on 20 March 1970 in Manhattan, New York City, USA. His parents are June Brody and David Rapaport. His mother, June, was a radio personality, while his father, David, was a radio program manager.

He has a brother named Eric Rapaport and an older half-sister, Claudia Lonow (née Rapaport), from his father's first marriage. After his parents divorced, Rapaport's mother married comic Mark Lonow.

The American entertainer attended Erasmus Hall High School in Brooklyn in the 1980s but was expelled. However, he later graduated from Martin Luther King High School in Manhattan.

Podcast career
