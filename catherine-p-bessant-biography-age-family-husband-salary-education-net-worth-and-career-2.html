Catherine P. Bessant Career

In 1982 Bessant started her career at Bank of America Corporation as a corporate banker. Sixteen years later she was assigned to the same company as the president of Community Development Banking and held that position until 2000. Cathy became Chair of Consumer Real Estate Banking at Bank of America Corporation in 1999. She also held the same role in the Florida market from 2000 to 2001 at that company.

Cathy held a Chief Marketing Officer position at Bank of America Corporation in 2001. She then became chair of Global Treasury Services and Global Product Solutions at that company in 2006. Cathy was named to chair Global Corporate Banking at Bank of America Corporation in 2009. She has been heading Global Technology and Operations since 2010, providing end-to-end infrastructure and operational services across the organization across almost 95,000 workers and contractors in over 35 nations. Cathy is Bank of America’s chief operations and technology officer and a member of the executive management team at the firm.

Catherine P. Bessant Awards and Achievements

Bessant was awarded the 2018 Distinguished Service Award at Charlotte by the University of North Carolina.
