Get To Know Mijntje Lupgens Age, Job, Height, Are You The One?

March 2024 · 5 minute read

The popular MTV dating show, Are You The One?, is back for its 9th season after its 3-year hiatus, in its new home, Paramount +. The 9th season of the show welcomed 22 hot singletons–11 males and 11 females–who will try to identify their predetermined perfect partner. Mijntje Lupgens is one of the participants in the new Paramount + show.

Get to know about Mijntje Lupgen’s new stint on Are You The One?, her age, family, job, dating status, and more in the wiki article below.

Mijntje Lupgens On Are You The One?

“A group of young singles looking for love mix and mingle in a luxury location as they try to identify their predetermined perfect mate with huge potential prize money on the line,” reads the official description of the show.

The 9th season of Are You The One? was shot in a luxurious villa in Gran Canaria and it featured cast coming from all around the world. The show has also gotten a new host, Kamie Crawford, whom viewers may have seen in Catfish.

Besides Mijntje Lupgens, the other cast members of the show are Anissa Aguilar, Brooke Rachman, Ciara “CC” Cortez, Courtney Rowe, Dew Anderson, Danielle Bonaparte, Jordanne Deveaux, Julia-Ruth Smith, Rosalyn “Roz” Odujebe, Taylor Kelly, Aqel Carson, Brendan Mosca, Clayton Carey, Eduardo Dickinson Jr., Hamudi Hasoon, Leo Svete, Michael “Mikey” Owusu, Nathan Grant, Ollie Andersen, Shamal “Samuel” Khan, and Will Gagnon.

Mijntje Lupgens first revealed her participation in the Paramount + series, Are You The One?, on January 6, 2022, through an Instagram post. She wrote, “I had to keep this a secret for a very long time but FINALLY I am allowed to share that I’ll be part of the upcoming @paramountplus series, ‘Are You The One?’ premiering January 18th! It has been such an honor to be part of the cast and having this incredible experience as the only Dutch member. Tune in to see if I find my Perfect Match.”

Mijntje Lupgens Dating Status

Mijntje Lupgens’ dating status was unclear as of Janaury 2023. However, given that she is on the dating show, Are You The One? to find her perfect mate, we guess she was single at the time of this writing.

Mijntje rarely features her family members in her socials. As a result of which, we don’t have much information on her family.

Mijntje Lupgens Age

Born in 1995, Mijntje Lupgens’s age was 27 in 2023.

Mijntje Lupgens Job

Mijntje Lupgens is a “dancer, photographer, and a creative director” according to her website mijntjelipgens.nl. She further writes on her website that she created a distinctive eye for different art forms that she uses to work within all disciplines through her experience in the dance industry.

Her Facebook handle shows that she currently works as an assistant choreographer and dancer at Talpa.

Mijntje has flown all over the world to dance.  Are You The One? star has danced with Dua Lipa during the EMAs in Seville Spain 2019, with Jason Derulo and Hailee Steinfield during the EMAs in Bilbao Spain 2018, with Helene Fisher during the Schlagerchampions in Germany, with Let’s Dance and the Friends of Amstel.

As a dancer, Mijntje has worked on TV shows like Dancing With The Stars Belgium 2019, The Voice of Holland, DanceSing, and more. She has participated in all seasons of Dance Dance Dance.

Mijntje’s music videos credits include Naomi Jon’s On Out Own, Rolf Sanchez’s Incredible, Luca Hanni’s Durch die nacht, Akon’s YES, and more.

In her interview with Women’s Health Magazine, Mijntje shared that the life of a dancer is not always easy. “As a freelancer, I depend on the work that is available at that moment in an industry where everything can suddenly be completely different. For major productions on television, I sometimes work 12-hour days for more than three months in a row. For a live show, I often train in three days to a week, and for larger shows, I train a month in advance,” the reality TV star said.

Whenever Mijntje has no major assignments, she provides workshops and lessons, choreographers an act, and takes lessons herself.

Besides dancing, Mijntje also works as an assistant choreographer with Galen Hooks from Los Angeles.

Mijntje Lupgens completed her education at Fontys School of the Arts, where she trained herself as a dance artist.
