We are teaming up with Drybar to give two lucky Hello! Fashion newsletter subscribers an exclusive gift 

    We’ve teamed up with Drybar to offer two lucky Hello! Fashion newsletter subscribers the chance to WIN a £500 gift voucher from the brand.

    In celebration of Drybar's London Fashion Week debut at the Roksanda show in February earlier this year, we are offering our newsletter subscribers the chance to win an exclusive gift. At Drybar, their philosophy is simple: Focus on one thing and be the best at it. For them, that’s blowdries. With that same goal in mind, they’ve created a line of professional quality products, tools & accessories to achieve and maintain the perfect blow-dry at home so you can feel happy and confident every day. Their extensive product line and services, created specifically for the perfect blow-dry, are available in the Harrods store in Knightsbridge, H Beauty stores across the UK.

    The iconic styling brand partnered with Roksanda for its AW23 LFW show in February. Lead hair stylist Mark Hampton created the looks for the show using Drybar tools and styling products, opting for individual styles to compliment the silhouettes of the collection. 

    “I wanted the hair to reflect each individual ROKSANDA Autumn/Winter 23 look. The days of when all the models had exactly the same hair on the catwalk are over,” Hampton explained, “For natural texture I was inspired by Queen Nefertiti and Grace Jones, so organic and interesting shapes that complemented the silhouettes of the collection. Straight hair focused on sleek and shiny hair which worked with the voluminous and feminine styles. The Drybar products & tools gave the ultimate in shine for beautifully styled hair for the show.”

    Hair was protected with Prep Rally Prime & Prep Detangler with sleek styles brushed through with the Super Lemon Drop Detangling Brush and straightened out with the Tress Press Digital Styling Iron. Textured styles were blown out using the best-selling Double Shot Blow-Dryer Brush and finished with the Money Maker Flexible Hold Hairspray for a high shine finish without the heaviness.
