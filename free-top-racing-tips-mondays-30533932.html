Monday's free horse racing tips by reigning Racing Post Naps champ Ayr Poet to strike

April 2024 · 2 minute read

AYR POET (5.25, nap) can do the business in Scotland on Monday for trainer Jim Goldie.

The eight year old has been knocking at the door and was a fast finishing second at Ayr a week ago.

He drops in trip as he returns to the track under useful apprentice William Pyle.

Pyle claims 7lb and Ayr Poet is a very well handicapped horse who will handle the soft ground.

He is best priced 11-4 (with most bookies) at the time of writing.

WHISKEY PRIEST (3.40, nb) left previous form behind to dead heat at Yarmouth last week and can follow up under a penalty in what looks a weak race.

ZARGA (8.20, trb) can complete the treble at Windsor tonight.

Sir Michael Stoute’s filly is still a maiden but it’s only a matter of time before she gets her head in front.

Jason Heavey is the reigning Racing Post Naps table champion and Chief Tipster on the Daily Star.
