M&amp;S launches latest Goodmove 2023 collection and it's our favourite yet

April 2024 · 5 minute read

Marks and Spencer launched its own activewear collection Goodmove in 2020, and it has gone from strength to strength ever since.

Goodmove fitness gear is available to shop for men and women, and has continued to expand over the years. Now, it has launched a new collection in time for Christmas and the New Year.

M&S' new Goodmove collection has dropped and it's perfect timing in advance of the New Year, which often sees people vow to embrace a healthier lifestyle in January.

There are almost 300 supportive womenswear Goodmove garments to shop at M&S, but it is the new drops as part of the Spring 2023, which has piqued our interest.

The latest launch includes fleece hoodies and quilted jackets made from M&S' Stormwear technology, which are waterproof and ideal for winter workouts, whether you are looking for layering to keep you warm when exercising outdoors, or to protect you from the elements en route to a class.

M&S also has a wide variety of supportive sports bras, which are comfortable and practical, and suitable for all body shapes - we can't get enough of them.

For those shopping for workout leggings, then look no further as M&S has quite the collection.

While some may be looking for leak proof creations, others may be in need of high waisted creations, and M&S has it all.

M&S' Goodmove range uses sweat-wicking fabric, which works to draw any moisture away from the body to help regulate your body temperature, even after your workout.
