Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker Take the Cake for the Best Halloween Couple Costume

August 2024 · 2 minute read

It’s a well-known fact Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker have a flair for over-the-top couple's costumes. After dressing up as the murderous Tiffy and Chucky dolls last year, the duo cemented themselves as the king and queen of Halloween yet again by dressing as another frightening famous couple: Beetlejuice and Lydia Deetz. On Monday, Kourtney posted a series of snaps, writing, “I’m the ghost with the most, babe” alongside the photos, which show their identical wedding looks from the 1988 cult classic Beetlejuice.

For her part, Kourtney slipped into a sheer, cherry red polka dot dress with a layered, voluminous tulle skirt and bold power shoulders paired with a coordinating veil and a bouquet of red roses. The Poosh founder styled her dark hair in a messy, tousled half-up, half-down hairstyle with forehead fringe and opted for dramatic red eye makeup, bronzy cheeks, and glossy lips.

Travis sported a spot-on look of Beetlejuice’s character with a burgundy tuxedo paired with a white ruffled dress shirt, a coordinating polka dot bow tie, and white dress shoes. The Blink 182 drummer rounded out his glam with corpse-like makeup and moss-green hair.

This Halloween, Kourtney is delivering a handful of the best costumes. Earlier this week, the soon-to-be mom dressed up as her younger sister, Kim, wearing the same floral Givenchy gown she wore to the 2013 Met Gala when she was pregnant with North.

"Freaky Friday," Kourtney captioned the post, which saw her pairing the maternity look with coordinating heeled sandals and a sleek low ponytail with a middle part.
