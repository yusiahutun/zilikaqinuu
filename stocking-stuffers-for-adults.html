The art of stocking stuffers may have been lost since you grew up and out of waking up at the crack of dawn to rip through your Christmas presents. But there’s really something to be said about stocking stuffers for adults, which are like the cherry on top when it comes to Christmas morning. And while stocking stuffers tend to be filled with a last-minute run to the grocery store, we’re here to give you a little more inspiration so that your stocking stuffers are more unique than, say, fun-sized Snickers bars leftover from Halloween.

These 40+ ideas will definitely help get you started on the best stocking stuffer ideas for adults — because stockings are not just about the kids! From gifts for music lovers to makeup for people always on the go, you’ll find a stocking stuffer for every grownup in your life who could a little more of that snack-sized holiday magic their stocking.
