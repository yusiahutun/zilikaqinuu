A hit sitcom, a well-established music career, a Broadway role and guest appearances: Reba McEntire’s net worth has it all. The country royalty has sold over 90 million copies throughout her life as a country singer, and she’s not stopping any time soon.

McEntire was discovered while singing the national anthem at a radio and captured the eye of country singer Red Steagell. After the stint, she signed to Polygram/Mercury Records and released her first single “I Don’t Want to Be a One Night Stand,” which initially failed to take off as it only peaked at number 88 on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. However, things started to pick up as she switched labels and released her albums Just a Little Love, My Kind of Country, Have I Got a Deal for You, Whoever’s in New England, What Am I Gonna Do About You, and more. She’s been nominated for 17 Grammys and won three.

In 2001, she starred in her own sitcom called Reba that ran for 6 years. She was previously married to producer and manager Narvel Blackstock for 26 years before they divorced in 2015. “Things started going south with my marriage.
