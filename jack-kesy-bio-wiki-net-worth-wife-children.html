Jack Kesy- Bio

Born on 27th August 1986 in New York City, New York under the birth sign Virgo, Jack Kesy is an actor. As per nationality, Kesy is American and belongs to White ethnicity. Despite born in the US, he spent his childhood in Italy and East Germany.

Moreover, Kesy attended Guildhall School of Music and Drama in London where he studied theater and drama. Initially, he served in the US Marine Corps and later built his career in acting.

In 2009, Kesy made his Holywood debut in short video, Emina. Following his debut, he started working in several movies and TV series. From 2014 to 2016, he portrayed the role of a philandering rock star, Gabriel Boliver in FX’s horror drama TV series, The Strain. The series featured Corey Stoll, Richard Sammel, and Kevin Durand.

Similarly, since 2017, he is working in the lead role of Roller Husser (Uncle Daddy’s nephew) in TNT’s TV series, Claws. He shares the screen with Niecy Nash, Karrueche Tran, and Carrie Preston.  As of 2018, Jack Kesy is working on an upcoming movie, Demented.

Deadpool

As we prior mentioned, Jack Kesy got huge recognition after playing the role of Black Tom Cassidy in the Marvel superhero film, Deadpool 2. He played his role alongside Josh Brolin, Morena Baccarin, Julian Dennison, T.J. Miller, Zazie Beetz, and many others.

The first part of the film, Deadpool grossed $783.1 Million on the box office whereas the second part, Deadpool 2 collected $785 Million on the box office. During the film Deadpool 2, there were rumors that Jack’s villain role has been cut from the movie, however, the co-writer of the movie, Rhett Resse confirmed the rumor to be false.
