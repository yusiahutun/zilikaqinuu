Longest FaceTime call ever according to the Guinness World Records

May 2024 · 5 minute read

You’ve probably come across lists of unique, enduring and weird Guinness World Records. People have won world records for the titles of longest moustaches, fastest typing with nose, biggest pillow fight and more. With technological advancements, there's no way technology will remain at the back of the queue in world records. One of the extraordinary records in this modern phenomenon is the record-breaking achievement of the longest FaceTime call ever.

FaceTime is an application for making voice and video calls over the internet. It's great for keeping in touch with your family and friends who use Apple devices. Facetime is available on devices made by Apple, such as Mac computers, iPads, and iPhones.

What is the longest FaceTime in the world?

Cisco Systems, Inc. holds the longest FaceTime call ever video world record. In 2020, Cisco had a 5-day conference call that lasted 138 hours and 58 minutes. They beat Selena and her friends' previously set FaceTime chatting record, which was 84 hours and 43 minutes.

However, a YouTuber, Amari Fields, recorded a video claiming that he had broken the longest FaceTime world record. But there's no official Guinness record that confirms he did it.

History of Guinness World Records of longest FaceTime calls

Other than Cisco Systems, there are several impressive FaceTime Call Guinness World Records, and they include:

First ever longest FaceTime call

The first world record for the longest FaceTime call on Snapchat dates back to 2015. An American woman named Selena and her friends chatted on FaceTime for 84 hours and 43 minutes. It held the first world record for longest FaceTime calls for several years until Cisco's.

Most romantic longest FaceTime call

Caitlyn and Alexis, a couple separated by distance, relied on FaceTime to infuse excitement into their relationship. Their dedication led to a remarkable achievement when they established a world record for one of the lengthiest FaceTime calls, clocking in at an astounding 88 hours, 53 minutes, and 20 seconds.

Longest FaceTime call for a refund

An angry customer from the United Kingdom made an extraordinary FaceTime call that etched its way into the Guinness World Records. Frustrated with his internet service provider, he insisted on a refund.

This conversation with the company's customer service representative spanned an impressive 20 hours. While this duration fell short of the lengths seen in other record-breaking FaceTime calls, its noteworthiness catapulted it to viral fame across social media platforms.

Do FaceTime calls have a time limit?

FaceTime has no time limit, and the calls can go on indefinitely until one person disconnects. But there are several issues that you have to worry about for particularly long calls.

Most notably, your device might overheat if you're doing other intensive tasks while it's plugged in, or your call might end up disconnecting by accident because of poor network connectivity, even if you don't intend it to.

What is the Guinness world record for longest phone call?

Today, the average phone call lasts only a few minutes and considering new communication trends, it is right to assume that phone calls will get even shorter over the years. Still, some people love a healthy chat and can talk over the phone for hours.

What is the Guinness world record for longest phone call? In 2012, Avery A. Leonard and Eric R. Brewster of Harvard University held a phone call for 46 hours, 12 minutes and 52 seconds. The two students weren't allowed to stop talking for more than 10 seconds but were given a 5-minute break to rest for every hour spent on the phone.

However, this phone call wasn't a regular chit-chat. It was a debut performance for installing a new students' cooperative, the Harvard Generalist. Other people were allowed to observe, hear the conversation, and even interact with Avery and Eric, thus inspiring new topics.

FAQs about FaceTime calls

  • Does FaceTime cost money? FaceTime is a free service for anyone with an Apple device that supports it. In addition, you can use it free internationally over Wi-Fi or cellular data.
  • Where was FaceTime 10 years ago? Apple CEO Steve Jobs launched FaceTime with the iPhone 4 on 7 June 2010.
  • Is it okay to stay on FaceTime all night? Yes, as long as you aren't holding the cell phone up against your ear, you shouldn't worry about health issues from prolonged exposure.
  • Who owns FaceTime? Apple developed FaceTime, which is primarily used on macOS, iPad and iOS devices. However, Windows and Android users with the latest Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome version can join Apple user's FaceTime.
  • Is FaceTime better than WhatsApp? Yes, FaceTime has crisper images and better-sounding audio than WhatsApp.
