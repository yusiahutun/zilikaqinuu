A Holistic Approach to Behavioral Healthcare

Agape Treatment Center, founded in 2016, is a behavioral healthcare company located in West Palm Beach, Florida. It is a cutting-edge leader in providing drug and alcohol addiction treatment services. The center offers a Day/Night, PHP, and Intensive Outpatient Program, or IOP drug rehab in Fort Lauderdale, designed to serve a growing number of clients best served in a more structured, nurturing, and managed environment.

Agape Treatment Center has a unique approach to healing that incorporates treatment, recovery, maintenance, prevention, philosophy, and psychology. Its “east meets west” approach focuses on helping clients achieve a balanced and rewarding lifestyle through integrating evidence-based services. The center’s program addresses physical, nutritional, chemical, environmental, emotional, social, spiritual, and lifestyle values and challenges.

Currently, Agape Behavioral Healthcare has three locations and offers all levels of care for individuals struggling with substance abuse and mental health. Its St. Lucie County facilities provide residential mental health and substance abuse detox residentially. In contrast, the center’s Fort Lauderdale location offers lower levels of care for mental health and substance use.

Agape Treatment Center’s program is designed to help individuals identify their underlying core issues and return to a place of wholeness in mind, body, and spirit. It is committed to helping individuals heal, recover, and transform from the inside out. With a strong emphasis on a holistic approach to behavioral healthcare, Agape Treatment Center is making a significant impact on the lives of its clients.

Agape Treatment Center’s mission is to provide exceptional long-term wellness to its clients and families. It offers care in various settings, including the privacy of the client’s homes, inpatient sober living spaces, and future Intensive Outpatient Centers and PHP. Agape Treatment Center is committed to delivering uncompromising care to each client in a Mental Health Residential Program setting that best addresses their unique needs.

A Transformative Healing Experience

Agape Treatment Center offers a top-tier, boutique experience for individuals seeking to heal and recover from addiction and mental health issues. The center’s commitment to customer service is unparalleled, and it strives to create a safe, inviting, and loving space for every person who walks through its doors. The team at Agape Treatment Center comprises highly-skilled clinical and medical experts who are up-to-date on the latest information and data.

This allows it to provide excellent, individualized care tailored to each person’s needs and desires. One of the hallmarks of Agape Treatment Center is its focus on individualized care. The clinical team works with each person to develop a customized treatment plan addressing their needs. The team is also trained in Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing (EMDR) and Rapid Resolution Therapy (RRT), which makes them a trauma-informed facility.

To support its community throughout the treatment experience, Agape Treatment Center’s Clinical Director wrote and published a 30-day workbook called WellNow. This supportive, evidence-based tool is designed to assist individuals in their healing journey. Every aspect of the Agape Treatment Center experience is carefully curated to be inviting and transformative. From the comfortable living environment to the chef’s delicious food, every detail is considered to ensure that individuals have the best possible experience.

Rehabilitation Services 

Agape Treatment Center offers a range of substance abuse and mental health rehabilitation services, including residential inpatient PHP drug rehabs in Fort Lauderdale, intensive outpatient programs, IOP drug rehab Fort Lauderdale option, and mental health rehabilitation. The programs are designed to provide patients with the necessary care and support to recover from their addiction and maintain sobriety.

The treatment programs at Agape Treatment Center are divided into two main categories: inpatient and outpatient programs. Residential treatment is typically for patients who need around-the-clock care due to co-occurring disorders or the severity of their addiction. On the other hand, PHP (partial hospitalization) programs provide intensive care for patients who require less structure than inpatient programs but still need daily monitoring by healthcare professionals to maintain sobriety while they go about their daily lives.

Mental Health Treatment at Agape

Agape Treatment Center provides comprehensive mental health treatment programs to individuals suffering from mental illness. With the prevalence of mental health disorders among adults, the licensed mental health clinicians at the center take these issues very seriously and strive to create a safe and nurturing environment for those seeking help. Its mental health treatment programs are designed to fully assess and diagnose individuals’ mental health disorders and provide them with a well-rounded treatment plan to address their specific needs.

The programs include various methodologies, such as:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Dialectical Behavioral Therapy
  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing
  • Rapid Resolution Therapy
  • Biofeedback Therapy
  • Relapse Prevention Training
  • Dual Diagnosis Treatment
  • Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy
  • Rational Living Therapy, Life Skills Training

Moreover, it offers Traditional Talk Therapy and other therapeutic methods like psychoeducation & psychotherapy, group therapy sessions, mental health & wellness, community living support, and case management. The licensed clinicians work closely with each individual to create a personalized treatment plan that addresses their unique mental health needs.

Agape Treatment Center believes that a comprehensive and well-rounded mental health plan is essential to achieving a healthy and productive life. By addressing the underlying mental health issues, individuals can learn healthy techniques to manage their mental health more effectively. With a focus on evidence-based services, Agape Treatment Center’s mental health treatment programs are designed to help individuals achieve balance and stability.
