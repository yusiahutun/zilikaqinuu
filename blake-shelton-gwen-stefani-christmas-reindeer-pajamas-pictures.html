Blake Shelton, Gwen Stefani + Family Wear Reindeer Pajamas

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Santa had eight reindeer but Blake Shelton's crew has 16. The singer smiles alongside girlfriend Gwen Stefani in a family photo that shows everyone wearing reindeer onesie pajamas, except for Mr. and Mrs. Claus.

The woman in the center of the picture looks like Shelton's mother Dorothy but Stefani's Instagram photo does not confirm anyone's identity. If you count the kids, adults and pets you get 16 furry reindeer, all happy to be celebrating the holidays together.

"Merrychristmas see u in #vegas tomorrow," Stefani captions her pic, referring to her Las Vegas residency at Planet Hollywood.

Shelton's socials were pretty quiet on Wednesday (Dec. 26) but Stefani filled in the blanks with several dozen clips at her Insta Stories feed that showed some crazy fun. The food, the dressing up, the singing and a little revelry — there was no shortage of laughs at her house this week.

The couple have spent much of the holiday season together and even released a new music video for Stefani's Christmas song "You Make It Feel Like Christmas" during which she pretends to be cheating on the country singer with Santa Claus. Shelton wrapped up his most recent season of the Voice earlier this month and will take a little time off before a 2019 tour. He's also believed to be working on new music but in the meantime he's been sharing covers of some of his favorite classic country songs from his childhood.

Earlier this month Shelton won the Entertainer of the Year Award during the annual Taste of Country Fan Choice Awards.
