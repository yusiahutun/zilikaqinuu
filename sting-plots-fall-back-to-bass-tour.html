Sting Plots Fall 'Back to Bass' Tour

May 2024 · 2 minute read

BOSTON (CelebrityAccess MediaWire)– Sting is preparing to hit the road this fall on his Back to Bass Tour throughout North America to commemorate the 25th anniversary of his solo career with stripped down renditions of some of his classic hits.

Sting will be touring in support of his forthcoming 3 CD boxed set collection Sting: 25 Years, slated for release on September 27, the tour features Sting backed by a 5-piece band in an intimate theatre setting.

The tour kicks off in Boston on October 21 at The Wang Theatre at the Citi Performing Arts Center, the tour will extend across the U.S. and Canada. Dates announced include Philadelphia, Washington DC, Toronto, Chicago, Detroit, Miami, Dallas, Houston, Denver, Phoenix, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Seattle and Vancouver. Additional dates will be announced in the coming weeks.

The tour is promoted by Live Nation and produced by RZO Entertainment, Inc.
