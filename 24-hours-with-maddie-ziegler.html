Maddie Ziegler Discusses Sia, Chandelier, Dance Moms, Pyramid, and More

April 2024 · 4 minute read

Media Platforms Design Team

7:00 AM: My mom wakes me up in the morning usually between 7:00 and 7:30 AM. First I pick out my outfit—I get in my dance clothes early so I am ready for the whole day. Then I do my hair and makeup, I love using MAC makeup, I use a lot of their products. I'm a beauty guru, I just love all of that stuff. We have a makeup artist for Dance Moms that touches us up and stuff but I love doing my own makeup, sometimes I do my friends too. My favorite is doing eye shadow and eyeliner.

7:30 AM I eat breakfast—I always have Captain Crunch with the Berries—that's the best thing, I eat that every morning.

8:00 AM Right now, we're living in apartments in L.A. for the show so I am being home-schooled with a tutor. I have school for about three hours in the morning.

12:30 PM On Wednesdays through Fridays we are getting ready for and filming Dance Moms, we go straight there after school. On Wednesdays, we film Pyramid for Dance Moms, we usually start around 12:30-1 and we finish at 3. Pyramid is a very short day because we have it for such a long time that we don't have as much time to dance.

3:00 PM After Pyramid we have off-camera rehearsal where we can get our dance routines done. Thursdays and Fridays we also have dance rehearsal, which is about three or four hours. We're only allowed to be on camera for 5 hours a day. I don't work with Sia every day, it depends what we do, whether we do performances or music videos so the schedule is weird but when we're off we always try to see each other in between. We just hang out, we went to brunch one time but for the most part I go to her house and we eat and watch TV. We started working together because she's actually a fan of our show Dance Moms. She tweeted me and said that she would love to work with me and she wants me to be in her new music video for "Chandelier" which was her new song at the time. My mom and my whole team saw it and told me, but I was like "Wait, what?!" I didn't know if it was real because it's not typical for a random offer to be in a music video from a celebrity. It's almost like if Beyonce was like, "Hey! Be in my music video!" But it was real and we contacted her and two weeks later we actually flew to L.A. and I filmed the video. We're like best friends, we're always hanging out and when I go to her house she's so sweet, she's like my sister or my mom, she is always caring for me. I love her a lot.

6:00 PM When we are doing filming for Dance Moms we finish around 6:00. Afterwards I'm always with my friends, we go to dinner or we eat in our apartments together.

8:00 PM I watch TV or hangout and watch Youtube videos. I'm always watching TV—my favorite show is Pretty Little Liars. I also like the Disney Channel too.

10:00 PM For filming we have to wear a lot more makeup than we usually would. At night, I have to go home and take off all the makeup and take my hair out from my bun and just get out of the dancewear and everything. Before I go to bed, I brush my teeth and take off all my makeup. I like to use Neutrogena's makeup remover wipes.
