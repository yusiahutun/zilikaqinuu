Aww Sam's Heart Emoji Donut Recipe

September 2024 · 2 minute read

So, as it turns out, I have a small obsession with the emoji heart and this is my THIRD emoji heart tutorial! Yes, you heard right, folks, I’ve done two other emoji heart projects (see heart clutch and BFF necklaces).

And when I found this heart-shaped donut pan, I knew another emoji donut project needed to happen! This is the perfect treat for your Valentine’s Day parties or to give to your best donut-loving gal pals. Who doesn’t love #emojisinthewild?!

This is one of my easier donut tutorials, and all it takes to transform this normal heart donut into an emoji is a little bit of fondant. Find the how-to below!

SUPPLIES

For the donuts:

2 cups flour
3/4 cups granulated sugar
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 eggs
3/4 cup skim milk
2 tablespoons butter, melted
White fondant
Gel food coloring in yellow
Heart-shaped donut pan (here)

For the glaze:

1/4 cup melted butter
1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 to 4 teaspoons hot water
Gel food coloring in pink
