Ashlee Simpson Ross Go to Bed with Me Video

May 2024 · 2 minute read

When singer-songwriter Ashlee Simpson Ross isn't working on new music or hanging with her family, she's carving out time from her day to complete her skin care routine. In this episode of Go to Bed with Me filmed at The West Hollywood EDITION, Ross shows us the skin care products she uses on a daily basis, from Chanel's Gentle Bi-Phase Eye Makeup Remover to Charlotte Tilbury's Instant Magic Facial Dry Sheet Mask.

Shop Ashlee's Routine

Ross centers her nightly skin care routine around hydration. "I like to keep it very hydrated," she says at the start of the episode. "I feel like there are times where I can get dry as it's getting colder in certain areas, so I definitely try to stay hydrated."

She does so by enlisting the help of trusted and true favorites, like the Bi-Phase eye makeup remover. "Back in the day when I was touring, I would wear serious eyeliner and lashes and everything, and I feel like this really helps," Ross says. She follows up with Clarins's Gentle Foaming Cleanser.

After washing off her cleanser, Ross applies L'Oréal's Pure Sugar Scrub to her lips. "For me, if I'm wearing a red lip or a lot of lip liner, I think it's important to scrub your lips," she says.

Watch the video above to see how Ross incorporates the rest of her favorite beauty products into her skin care routine.
