URL shorteners are both useful and convenient, and while there are lots of powerful contenders in the field, sometimes an alternative shortener is what you need. From URLs that let you easily keep track of where they go to links with funny names, there are all sorts of these for all kinds of needs.

URLify allows you to make super shady URLs from any link. Just paste a URL in and watch it become something that looks like a link nobody should ever click on:

http://6h2.xyz/cuck_ntok_heroin-playtime_ttrjn_crippled-children_d0wnload [Broken URL Removed]

Whether you want to gross out your friends or just run a little experiment to see if people actually pay attention to the links they're clicking, this generator will get you there in a hurry.

Be careful about using this on a network where you're monitored (or even if you're at home and not using a VPN) as the URLs could raise suspicion from those in charge!

As a bonus, the site also offers a "Trumpify" feature for URLs. If this is something you'd be interested in, go to town.
